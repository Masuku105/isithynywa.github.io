ABOUT US

Isthunywa Projects PTY LTD was established in 2017 and is 100% black owned. It was established to provide construction and Engineering services and supplies to both the public and private sectors in the South African market. The company consist of a dynamic and energetic management team of a multi skilled South Africans who provide a firm foundation to the Company.

ISTHUNYWA PROJECTS PTY LTD binds itself to be a service provider of choice by:

Developing high standards and a dynamic business culture. Timeous and efficient service delivery. The development of management and employees to ensure that they reach their full potential both academically and personally. Implementing and maintaining workable structures while adopting a personal approach toward clients. Co-operating and coordinating with all Client departments and contractors to achieve goals of projects. Professional and Cost Effective attitude.

Goals

To be recognized by both internal and external markets as a dynamic top-notch enterprise. To build up a company known in Africa and internationally for its consistency, reliability and integrity. To maintain a profitable business with a steady growth, in order to be able to continue creating opportunities for people.

Values

Isthunywa pty ltd is committed to high-quality, affordable services displaying sustainable values and using locally produced products, suppliers and resources.

Long Term Goals

To be an organization that will not be only recognized in South Africa but in the whole country. Provide training opportunities for students in the built environment and Engineering sector.
